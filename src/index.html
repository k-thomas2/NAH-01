What is AAHA?

AAHA-Accredited Hospitals: Champions for Excellent Care

Did you know that accredidation for animal hospitals is voluntary? Surprising, isn't it? Nearly 60% of pet owners believe that their pet's veterinary hospital is accredited when it is not. In acutality, only 12-15% of animal hospitals have gone through the accredidation evaluation process by the American Animal Hospital Association (AAHA).
